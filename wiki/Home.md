# Nerospace Wiki

**Nerospace** is a space-exploration / tech-progression mod for Minecraft (Java Edition), built on
**NeoForge for Minecraft 26.1**. Mine alien materials, build machines, craft rockets, travel to other
worlds, survive their airless atmospheres with oxygen gear, and eventually **terraform** a dead planet
into livable ground.

> ⚠️ Nerospace is an early work in progress — features, balance, and art may all still change. This
> wiki is a living document and will be expanded over time.

## Getting started

1. Mine **Nerosium Ore** in the Overworld and smelt **Raw Nerosium** into **Nerosium Ingots**.
2. Build a **Nerosium Grinder** to double your ore yield via **Nerosium Dust**.
3. Progress to **Nerosteel** and **Xertz Quartz** on the Greenxertz planet, then craft a **Rocket**.
4. Pack an **Oxygen Suit** and set up **Oxygen Generators** — the planets and the station are airless.
5. Late game: build a **Terraformer** to convert a planet's surface into breathable, vibrant land.

## Dimensions at a glance

| Dimension | Reached by | Sky | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Overworld | — | normal | Source of Nerosium. |
| **Greenxertz** | Tier-2 rocket | day/night + sun | Green planet; Nerosteel + Xertz Quartz. Airless. |
| **Cindara** | Tier-2 rocket | space starfield | Volcanic moon; Cindrite. Airless. |
| **Orbital Station** | Tier-1 rocket | space starfield | A single platform in orbit. Airless/void. |

All Nerospace dimensions are **airless** — without a breathable zone (an Oxygen Generator, a Launch
Pad, terraformed ground, or a full Oxygen Suit) you will run out of oxygen and suffocate.
