# Block of Glacite

Compact storage for nine Glacite gems.

## Overview

A pale, ice-blue storage block of the Glacira crystal.

## Obtaining

- **Craft:** fill a 3×3 grid with **Glacite**.
- **Unpack:** craft the block alone to get **9 Glacite** back.

## Details

- ID: `nerospace:glacite_block`
- Tool: pickaxe, iron tier · Drops: itself
